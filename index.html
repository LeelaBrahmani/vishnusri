    About Us

    • Casa Kitchen was created to serve delicious food to the customers who would like to Enjoy, Relish and Ignite their taste buds.
    • Casa Kitchen serves both Veg & Non-Veg, with a limited specialised menu and doesn’t serve all generic items unlike normal restaurants.
    • Casa Kitchen currently manages both Takeaway & Catering services and partnered with Swiggy & Zomato delivery partners.
    • Casa Kitchen main objective is to take up bulk orders in Kilo’s that would cater to Family & Social gatherings / events
    • Casa Kitchen serves delicious Hyderabadi Dum Biryani & Mouth Watering Starter dishes that would light up your evenings.
